Engine & Diagnostics

Complete engine diagnostics, repair, and performance services

Check Engine Light Repair

Advanced computer diagnostics to identify and resolve engine warning lights. We use factory-level scan tools to read trouble codes and pinpoint the root cause before recommending repairs.

Engine Repair

Complete engine repair services from minor fixes to major overhauls. Our technicians handle head gaskets, valve jobs, timing chains, and full engine replacements on domestic and import vehicles.

Timing Belt Replacement

Critical timing component replacement to prevent costly engine damage. We follow manufacturer-recommended intervals and replace related components like water pumps and tensioners.

Catalytic Converter Replacement

Catalytic converter diagnostics, repair, and replacement. We verify converter failure with emissions testing and install OEM-grade replacements that meet Texas inspection standards.

Spark Plugs Replacement

Spark plug replacement for improved performance and fuel economy. We use manufacturer-specified plugs and inspect ignition wires and coils during service.

Mass Airflow Sensor Replacement

MAF sensor diagnostics and replacement for proper engine operation. A failing sensor causes rough idle, poor acceleration, and reduced fuel efficiency.

Fluid Leaks Detection

Expert detection and repair of oil, coolant, transmission fluid, and other leaks. We use UV dye tracing and pressure testing to locate the source accurately.

Digital Vehicle Inspection

Comprehensive digital vehicle inspection with photos and a detailed report sent to your phone. See exactly what your vehicle needs so you can make informed decisions.

Brakes & Suspension

Brake system service and suspension repair for a safe, smooth ride

Brake Pads

Front and rear brake pad replacement with quality ceramic or semi-metallic pads. We also inspect rotors, calipers, and brake fluid during every brake service.

Shocks and Struts Replacement

Shock and strut replacement for improved ride comfort and vehicle handling. Worn shocks increase stopping distance and cause uneven tire wear.

Ball Joints Service and Repair

Ball joint inspection, replacement, and repair. Failed ball joints cause loose steering, clunking noises, and uneven tire wear that worsens over time.

Suspension Service and Repair

Complete suspension diagnostics, service, and repair including control arms, tie rods, sway bar links, and bushings. We restore factory ride quality and handling.

Wheel Alignments

Precision wheel alignment to prevent uneven tire wear and improve handling. We perform 4-wheel alignments using computerized equipment and adjust to factory specifications.

AC & Climate Control

Keep cool in the San Antonio heat with expert AC service

Auto AC Service and Repair

Complete automotive AC system service and repair. From simple recharges to full system overhauls, we restore cold air performance for the San Antonio summer.

AC Diagnosis

Expert AC system diagnostics using refrigerant leak detection, pressure testing, and temperature analysis to identify issues quickly and accurately.

AC R-134a Recharge

Refrigerant recharge to restore cold air performance. We evacuate the old refrigerant, check for leaks, and recharge to the exact manufacturer specification.

Compressor Replacement

AC compressor diagnosis and replacement services. We install quality compressors with new receiver driers and expansion valves for reliable cooling.

Evaporator Replacement

AC evaporator core replacement and repair. A leaking evaporator causes weak cooling and a musty smell inside the cabin.

AC Control Module Replacement

AC control module diagnostics and replacement for automatic climate control systems that are not responding correctly.

Ambient Temperature Sensor Replacement

Ambient temperature sensor diagnostics and replacement. A faulty sensor causes incorrect climate control behavior and dash display errors.

Blower Motor Repair & Replacement

Blower motor diagnostics, repair, and replacement. If you hear squealing or your fan only works on certain speeds, the blower motor or resistor may need service.

Heater Core Replacement

Heater core service and replacement for proper cabin heating. Signs of a failing heater core include foggy windows, a sweet smell, and damp carpet on the passenger side.

Cabin Air Filter

Cabin air filter replacement for clean, healthy air inside your vehicle. We recommend replacing your cabin filter every 15,000 to 20,000 miles.

Preventive Maintenance

Regular maintenance to keep your vehicle running at its best

Oil Change & Filter Replacement

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes with premium filters. Regular oil changes are the single most important thing you can do to extend engine life.

Auto Tune Ups

Complete engine tune-up services for optimal performance. Includes spark plugs, air filter, fuel filter, and system inspections tailored to your vehicle and mileage.

30K/60K/90K Service

Factory-recommended milestone maintenance services. We follow your manufacturer's schedule to keep your warranty valid and prevent expensive repairs down the road.

Belts and Hoses

Serpentine belt, drive belt, and radiator hose inspection and replacement. Cracked or worn belts can snap without warning, leaving you stranded.

Engine Air Filter

Engine air filter replacement for improved performance and fuel economy. A dirty air filter restricts airflow and makes your engine work harder than it should.

Coolant System Services

Radiator flush, coolant replacement, and cooling system service. Fresh coolant protects against overheating in summer and freezing in winter.

Power Steering Fluid Change

Power steering fluid flush and replacement to maintain smooth, responsive steering. Contaminated fluid causes whining noises and stiff steering.

Water Pump Replacement

Water pump diagnostics and replacement to prevent engine overheating. A failing water pump leaks coolant and can cause catastrophic engine damage if ignored.

State Inspection

Official Texas state vehicle safety and emissions inspection. Walk-ins welcome. We can also address any issues found during inspection to get your vehicle compliant.

Electrical Systems

Modern vehicle electrical diagnostics and repair

Alternator Service and Replacement

Alternator testing, service, and replacement. A failing alternator causes dim headlights, dead batteries, and warning lights on your dashboard.

Alternator Testing

Comprehensive alternator output and performance testing. We verify charging voltage and amperage under load to determine if replacement is needed.

Headlight Restoration

Headlight lens restoration for improved nighttime visibility and appearance. Oxidized headlights reduce light output by up to 80 percent and are an inspection failure.

Windshield Wipers Repair/Replacement

Windshield wiper motor repair and blade replacement. Good wipers are critical for safety in San Antonio's sudden downpours.

Transmission & Drivetrain

Transmission service, repair, and drivetrain maintenance

Transmission Repair

Transmission diagnostics and repair for shifting issues, slipping, delayed engagement, and warning lights. We service both automatic and manual transmissions.

Transmission Fluid Inspection

Transmission fluid inspection and service for automatic and manual transmissions. Clean fluid extends transmission life and prevents costly rebuilds.

Clutch Repair/Replacement

Clutch disc, pressure plate, and flywheel services for manual transmission vehicles. We also handle hydraulic clutch components like slave cylinders and master cylinders.

Tires

Tire services to keep you safe on the road

Seasonal Tire Replacement

Seasonal tire changeover and new tire installation. We mount, balance, and set pressures to manufacturer specs. Ask about our tire disposal and recycling.

Tire Pressure Checks

Tire pressure inspection and adjustment to manufacturer specs. Properly inflated tires improve fuel economy, extend tire life, and keep you safe on the road.

Specialty Services

Specialized auto care services for unique needs

Diesel Service and Repair

Expert diesel engine diagnostics, service, and repair. We work on Duramax, Cummins, Power Stroke, and other diesel platforms for trucks and SUVs.

Hybrid Vehicle Service and Repair

Specialized service for hybrid vehicles including battery health checks, regenerative braking systems, and high-voltage component maintenance.

Auto Body

Auto body repair and cosmetic services including dent repair, bumper restoration, and paint touch-ups to keep your vehicle looking its best.

Frame Repair

Vehicle frame inspection and repair services. We straighten and reinforce damaged frames to restore structural integrity after collisions.

Car Rodent Damage Repair

Repair of wiring, hoses, and components damaged by rodents. Rodent damage is more common than you think and can cause electrical shorts, no-start conditions, and coolant leaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our auto repair services

How long does a typical repair take?

Most routine services like oil changes and brake pad replacements are completed the same day, often within a few hours. Larger repairs such as engine or transmission work may take 1-3 business days depending on parts availability. We always provide a time estimate upfront before starting any work.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes. Every repair we perform is backed by our industry-leading 3-year/36,000-mile warranty. This covers both parts and labor, giving you confidence that your vehicle is protected long after you leave our shop.

What brands and vehicle types do you service?

We service all major domestic and import brands including Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Honda, Nissan, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and more. Our technicians are trained to work on cars, trucks, SUVs, and light-duty diesel vehicles. We also handle hybrid vehicle service and repair.

Do you offer financing for major repairs?

Yes, we partner with Affirm, Klarna, and Sunbit to offer flexible financing options. You can split the cost of your repair into manageable monthly payments with quick approval, often in minutes. Ask our service advisors for details.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can book an appointment online using the "Book Now" button on our website, call us directly at (726) 232-8622, or stop by our shop at 4715 N Stahl Park Suite 106, San Antonio, TX 78217. Walk-ins are welcome for quick services like oil changes and state inspections.
