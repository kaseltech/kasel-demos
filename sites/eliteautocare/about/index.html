Our Story and Mission

Built on integrity, driven by service

Founded with a commitment to honesty and transparency, Elite Auto Care has become a trusted name in San Antonio, TX auto repair. We're a military-owned and operated business that values reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction above all. Our skilled ASE Master Certified technicians take pride in offering thorough inspections, accurate diagnostics, and dependable repairs for all vehicle types, ensuring you stay safe on the road.

Customers choose Elite Auto Care because we provide a rare balance of expert service, personalized care, and competitive pricing. Every repair is backed by a strong 3-year/36,000-mile warranty on qualifying parts and labor, so you can trust in the quality of our work. Whether you drive a European luxury car, a domestic truck, a diesel, or even an EV, our team has the experience and tools to get the job done right the first time here in San Antonio, TX.
