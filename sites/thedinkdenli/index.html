Your Home Court on Long Island

The Dink Den was founded with a simple mission: to bring world-class pickleball to Long Island. Our facility features professional-grade courts, top-tier equipment, and a welcoming atmosphere for players of all skill levels. From beginners looking to learn the game to seasoned competitors seeking competitive play, The Dink Den is your home court.
