About The Dink Den

Redefining the pickleball experience on Long Island.

Our Story

Built for the Love of the Game

The Dink Den Pickleball Club was founded with a simple mission: to bring world-class pickleball to Long Island. Our facility features professional-grade courts, top-tier equipment, and a welcoming atmosphere for players of all skill levels. From beginners looking to learn the game to seasoned competitors seeking competitive play, The Dink Den is your home court.

Facility Photo

The Facility

World-Class, Right Here on Long Island

Our state-of-the-art facility features 6 professional-grade indoor courts with tournament-quality surfaces, LED lighting, and full climate control so you can play comfortably year-round.

Beyond the courts, The Dink Den offers a welcoming lounge area, a pro shop stocked with premium paddles and gear, and a community built around the fastest-growing sport in America.
